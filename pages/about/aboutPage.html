Where it all began

I guess you could say it all began when I decided to fulfil a dream of mine which was to build a game. So I downloaded Unity, blender and visual studio to build this game. Unity uses the C# programming language which i knew was no easy task for me to learn. Through much struggle and many headaches I slowly became more comfortable and familiar with C# and soon found myself enjoying the process of programming and seeing the results of it. I thought to myself ‘what else could I do?’ and began to research more and more.
After that I decided to learn HTML, CSS3 as well as Javascript. This really unlocked more for me and it became my obsession to learn more and found that this is really something that suits me. I find myself to be a very patient, self driven, well focused and hard working person which fits the programmer life well.

More personal

I am originally a freelance Audio Engineer and musician, creating sound effects and writing music for games which is a big passion of mine. Ever since I was little I have had a fascination with games, the creation of a virtual world which can really immerse you.
I am also an athletic person on the side who actively does calisthenics to stay fit. Other activities I enjoy are swimming, bouldering, bodyboarding, skating and long walks. I feel it is important to keep active and healthy to have a clear mind and keep motivated.
